About


KotobaWeb is an extension of work I've done for my Discord Bot. In addition to the features here on the website, the bot has SRS, saving/loading, leaderboards, more question categories, plus other Japanese-related functions including dictionary searching, kanji searching, verb deconjugation, furigana rendering, and more.

The site was launched on 2/4/2018 and is under construction.
